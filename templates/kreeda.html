KREEDA-2k19



Kreeda 2k19 is the annual national level Sports fest of B V C Engineering College and is definitely one of the best. It is a 2-day festival held in March. Teams from all over Andhra Pradesh make it a point to participate in the events of Kreeda. The huge campus of B V C Engineering College has made the fest a huge hit.




Events


  • Cricket (Men)
  • Volley Ball (Men)
  • Kabaddi (Men)
  • Foot Ball (Men)
  • Shuttle (Men & Women)
  • Table Tennis (Men & Women
  • Throw Ball (Women)
